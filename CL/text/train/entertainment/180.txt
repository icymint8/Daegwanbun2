Meet Team USA's Olympic athletes for the 2024 Paris Games
Hundreds of the nation's top athletes are headed to Paris as part of Team USA in the 2024 Olympics. More than 590 athletes will compete in dozens of athletic events, including gymnastics, swimming, track and field sports and more. The Paris Games will begin on Friday, July 26, and last until Sunday, Aug. 11. Here's the full list of athletes representing Team USA at the 2024 Olympic Games. One of Team USA Men's Basketball players will have a distinct honor: LeBron James, who will become the first U.S. men's basketball player tocarry the American flag during the opening ceremony. One of Team USA Tennis' competitors will be one of the two American athletes carrying the flag during the opening ceremony: Coco Gauff, the first U.S. tennis player to have that honor.
